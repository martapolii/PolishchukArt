ARTIST BIO

  • 07.06.1974 - born in Lviv, Ukraine
  • 1981 - 1989 - studied at Lviv secondary school # 87
  • 1985 - 1988 - studied at Lviv art O. Novakivsky school
  • 1989 - 1994 - studied at Lviv Trush college of decorative and applied art, department of artistic technologies in wood
  • 1994 - 2000 - studied at Lviv University of Arts, department of Restoration, Conservation and Preservation of monumental and decorative applied art
  • 2000 - present time - owner of company Excalibur Art Restoration
  • Since 2007 - Vice-President of Ukrainian Association of Visual Artists of Canada
